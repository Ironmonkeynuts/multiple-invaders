How to Play

Alien ships are labelled with numbers. Fire number bombs that are factors of the ship number.

Controls

  • Left Arrow = move left
  • Right Arrow = move right
  • Spacebar = fire
  • 1, 2, 3, 5 = choose bomb

Bomb Rules

  • Not a factor = 0 damage
  • Factor = 1 damage
  • Prime factor = 2 damage
