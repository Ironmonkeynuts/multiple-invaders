Level 1: Factors Attack

Destroy the ships by firing factor bombs.

Prime factors do extra damage.

How to Play

Each alien ship is labelled with a number. Fire a bomb that is a factor of that number to damage it.

Controls

  • Left Arrow = move left
  • Right Arrow = move right
  • Spacebar = fire
  • 1, 2, 3, 5 = choose bomb

Damage Rules

  • Not a factor = 0 damage
  • Factor = 1 damage
  • Prime factor = 2 damage

Enemy Numbers

10, 12, 20, 24, 30
